NAGARAJA G S

Professor and Associate Dean

Dr. Nagaraja G.S is the Associate Dean and Professor at RV College of Engineering. He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Dr MGR University, Chennai, India (2011). With over 30 years of teaching, 19 years of research, and 6 years in skill development, he has served as Lecturer, Associate Professor, and Professor in the Dept of CSE at RVCE, Bangalore.
